ABOUT US

We are a small longboard store that was founded in 2014 by Kai Grapow. We strive to push the limits of longboarding one product at a time. We have many amazing team riders holding up the flag, including our founder, Kai Grapow, Chris Kirsten, Tom Curd and Conna Niemand.
