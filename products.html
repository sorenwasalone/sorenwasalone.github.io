BOARDS

OMEN Barfight


Description

The Barfight is an innovative topmount freeride and downhill board. Featuring flushed-mounting, our signature accelerated rocker, wheelflares and radial concave, you will have all you need to bust out slides with your feet sitting comfortably. Note: Taller riders should consider the Omen Airship, which has more standing room and a longer platform.



Specifications

  • Length: 34.5"
  • Width: 9.5"
  • Wheelbase: 22.5-26.5"
  • Price: R1800


OMEN Barbarian


Description

The Barbarian is referred to as a wooden Matrix (In reference to our first carbon fiber board, the Carbon Matrix). It features a 1 inch drop, and symmetrical standing platform to provide a lower and more stable ride height. It is a great deck for commuting, sliding, and learning. The Drops provide reference points to let you know where your feet are at all times. If you want the most board control possible, this is the board for you.



Specifications

  • Length: 38.5"
  • Width: 9.75"
  • Wheelbase: 30-32"
  • Price: R1900

OMEN Riot


Description

The Riot is a bi-directional freeride focused deck. Featuring a 1/3" of rocker, and 1/2" of concave, with a length running Subtle "W" this board has the most organic feel that Omen has to offer. Toesides are stress-free when you realize you always have a feature to push against, and the rocker of the deck cups your feet, so foot slip is forgotten. Take this deck to the Streets and start a Riot.



Specifications

  • Length: 36"
  • Width: 9.75"
  • Wheelbase: 23-27"
  • Price: R1800



WHEELS

FREE Thoughts


                

Description

Free Thoughts are wheels that are poured in the new 79a PLATINUM Formula. The Platinum Formula is known for long slides, minimal resistance, and slow wear. This wheel doesn't dump thane like some of the others, but it lasts longer than any wheel we have developed to date. Combined with the fully side-set bearing seat and the elliptical lips, this is a forgiving shape that is catch free on even the roughest of pavements. Besides coming stone-ground, this wheel is perfect for younger and beginner riders to be using because of the easy slide characteristics, and flat-spot resistant tendencies allow you to learn to slide easily!

Specifications

  • Contact Patch: 43mm
  • Durometer: 79a
  • Price: R560

FREE Quincys


                

Description

The Quincy is 65mm tall and is the little brother to the Thoughts. Coming stone-ground with a center-set bearing seat gives this wheel amazing slide qualities, and amazing effortless slides. Topmounts and boards with less wheel clearance will love the Quincy because all of the performance is packed into a smaller size, which will allow you to tune into your setup!



Specifications

  • Contact Patch: 65mm
  • Durometer: 79a
  • Price: R530

FREE Ballins


                

Description

Free Ballins are a round lipped wheel intended for fast freeriding and sliding. The "hubset" bearing seat combines the release of a side-set wheel with the traction of an offset wheel. The inside lip is fully supported giving the best release and hook-up at high speeds. The "Money" formula for this wheel is an intuitive sliding formula that is second to none, giving you the confidence to let it hangout there.



Specifications

  • Contact Patch: 70mm
  • Durometer: 80a
  • Price: R720
